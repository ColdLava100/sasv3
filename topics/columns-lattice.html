📐 Columns, Rows & Lattice ROLES ▶

Columns/Rows = for Crosstab only. Lattice Columns/Rows = for Charts only. They are not interchangeable.

📋 Crosstab

Columns → Category spreads across top (Q1, Q2, Q3...)
Rows → Category goes down the side (Sports, Toys...)
Q1Q2Q3
Sports$250$150$200
Toys$80$120$90

📈 Charts

Lattice Columns → Split chart into side-by-side mini-charts 📐📐
Lattice Rows → Split chart into stacked rows of mini-charts 📐
Sports
Toys
LATTICE COLUMNS → side by side

🧠 Ultimate Memory Trick:
Crosstab: Columns = headers across top, Rows = labels down the side
Charts: Lattice Columns = side-by-side panels, Lattice Rows = stacked panels
"Rows go down 📐, Columns go across 📐📐"
